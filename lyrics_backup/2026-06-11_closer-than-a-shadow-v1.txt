[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I wanna be closer than a shadow on your wall
Catching every whisper right before you fall
I feel the moonlight pull me in when you are near
A gentle fire burning everything I fear

[Verse 1]
The coffee cooling on the table made of pine
Your fingers tracing lazy circles into mine
We left the heavy roads and city lights away
Just stealing moments at the ending of the day

[Pre-Chorus]
The temperature is rising in this quiet room
I smell the rainfall mixing with the midnight bloom
I pull you in and let the world outside erase
Lost in the rhythm and the warmth of this embrace

[Chorus]
Hold me closer than a shadow in the night
Tangled up together in the fading light
I want to memorize the rhythm of your soul
And pull you closer as we lose all our control

[Post-Chorus]
Closer than a shadow, closer to the flame
Falling in the magic when you say my name

[Verse 2]
A single candle flickering against the glass
We watch the quiet moments beautifully pass
The way you look at me is breaking my defense
A gentle longing that is suddenly intense

[Bridge]
We slow it down and let the heavy world dissolve
A beautiful equation that we finally solve
The atmosphere is turning into something grand
Just tracing rivers on the palm of your soft hand

[Spoken]
